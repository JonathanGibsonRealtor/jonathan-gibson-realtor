About

Jonathan Gibson Realtor

Jonathan Gibson is a real estate professional based in the Wilmington area, working with buyers, sellers, and investors across New Hanover, Pender, and Brunswick Counties.

A large part of his business centers around luxury, waterfront, and coastal properties—where pricing, positioning, and negotiation matter more than ever. From Intracoastal Waterway homes to established neighborhoods and investment opportunities, Jonathan approaches each deal with a clear strategy and a focus on long-term value.

While much of his work is in higher-end and waterfront homes, Jonathan works with clients at all stages—first-time buyers, move-up sellers, and investors—bringing the same level of attention and discipline to every transaction.
